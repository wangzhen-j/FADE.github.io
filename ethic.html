Ethic

All of our videos have been authorized by the Wuhan University and we own all videos. We recruit seven volunteers to help us throw the objects from buildings. We inform them in advance that their faces and hands might appear in the video and obtain their consent. In addition, we also delete other videos that might violate the ethical principles of the NeurIPS.
