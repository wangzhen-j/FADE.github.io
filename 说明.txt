FADE Consists of:
we collect videos in diverse weather conditions, light, conditions, scenes

we collect nine kinds of objects as follows: clothes, basins, shoes, kitchen waste, water balloons, books, spitballs, bottles, and packaging bags.

we consider three different camera angles: 30°, 45°, and 60°.

we provide videos with four resolutions: 1280 × 720, 1920 × 1080, 2560 × 1440, and 2592 × 1520.

we also collect rich background video data with diverse backgrounds

Area ranges and proportions of the falling objects in our FADE dataset
